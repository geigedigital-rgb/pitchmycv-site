## Page mapping

- `/` -> resume checker/scanner/analyzer/score + ATS checker intent
- `/tools/ats-checker` -> ATS resume checker/scanner/test/compatibility + long-tail checks
- `/resume-job-match` -> resume job match + match job description + fit score
- `/resume-interview-chances` -> interview chances/probability/score
- `/resume-keyword-optimization` + `/resume-keywords-for-ats` -> keyword checker/optimization intent
- Article pages under root:
  - `/how-to-pass-ats-resume`
  - `/how-to-check-resume-ats-score`
  - `/what-ats-score-is-good`
  - `/how-to-match-resume-with-job-description`
  - `/resume-keywords-for-ats`

## On-page implementation rules

- Keep one primary intent per page in title and H1.
- Use secondary variants in H2 and paragraph copy naturally.
- Avoid keyword stuffing; every term should fit context.
- Maintain internal links between money pages and article pages.
- Keep canonical tags and sitemap entries in sync.
- Preserve readable UX copy for human conversion, not only search bots.
