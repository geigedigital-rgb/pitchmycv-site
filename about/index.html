About Pitch My CV

Pitch My CV helps job seekers create resumes that are readable by applicant tracking systems and compelling to recruiters.

We focus on practical improvements: parse-safe formatting, keyword relevance to job descriptions, and stronger achievement bullet points.

Our goal is simple: reduce auto-rejections and increase interview opportunities with transparent, actionable guidance.

What Pitch My CV does in practice

We build resume optimization resources for one job seeker problem: getting qualified candidates past ATS screening without making resumes unreadable to recruiters. The product combines a resume checker, role-specific examples, keyword guidance, and long-form career pages built around practical application workflows.

Our content is designed to help users diagnose structure issues, identify missing role language, and improve bullet clarity before they apply to jobs. We prioritize actionable guidance over generic career advice.

What we value

  • Clear recommendations, not generic advice
  • Job seeker privacy and control over content
  • Practical resume outcomes over vanity metrics

How we review and publish advice

Pitch My CV publishes resume and ATS guidance using a repeatable editorial workflow: identify common failure points in resume structure, map them to the actual language used in job descriptions, and convert the findings into checklists, examples, and role-specific guides.

We do not promise interviews or job offers. We focus on the parts of the process we can help improve directly: parsing reliability, keyword relevance, recruiter readability, and better self-auditing before an application is submitted.

Editorial standards

  • We align guidance with real ATS parsing behavior and recruiter scanning patterns.
  • We publish role-specific examples instead of one-size-fits-all resume advice.
  • We update pages when product flows, legal details, or content assumptions materially change.
  • We keep legal, privacy, and contact routes visible so users can understand who operates the service.

Company and contact signals

Pitch My CV is operated by MARYSCHAKGROUP LTD, registered in the United Kingdom. We maintain public legal and privacy documentation, product support email routes, and service documentation pages so users can verify how the service works and who is responsible for it.
