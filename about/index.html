About Pitch My CV

Pitch My CV helps job seekers create resumes that are readable by applicant tracking systems and compelling to recruiters.

We focus on practical improvements: parse-safe formatting, keyword relevance to job descriptions, and stronger achievement bullet points.

Our goal is simple: reduce auto-rejections and increase interview opportunities with transparent, actionable guidance.

What we value

  • Clear recommendations, not generic advice
  • Job seeker privacy and control over content
  • Practical resume outcomes over vanity metrics
