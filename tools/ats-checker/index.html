How our ATS checker works

Our scanner mimics the exact logic used by enterprise hiring software like Taleo, Greenhouse, and Workday.

1. Formatting Parse Test

We attempt to extract your text. If you use columns, tables, or complex designs, we'll flag exactly where the parser fails to read your experience.

2. Keyword Density Scan

We compare the vocabulary in your document against standard industry requirements to find missing hard skills and buzzwords.

3. Action Verb Analysis

We detect weak, passive language (e.g., "responsible for") and suggest strong, metric-driven verbs that algorithms score highly.
