Resume Keyword Optimization

Use this simple process to match job keywords without making your resume sound robotic.

Step 1: Extract role-critical terms

Read the job post and list repeated terms. Focus on hard skills, tools, and role-specific verbs.

Step 2: Map terms to real experience

Only use terms you can prove with real work examples. This keeps your resume honest and stronger in interviews.

Step 3: Place keywords strategically

Add top keywords to Summary, Skills, and recent Experience bullets. Keep each sentence clear and specific.

Step 4: Validate and iterate

Run your resume in the ATS checker, then fix weak sections with this mistakes guide.
