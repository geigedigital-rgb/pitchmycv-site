Marketing Manager Resume Example & Guide

Marketing resumes work best when they show clear channel ownership and real growth metrics. This guide helps you format that clearly for ATS and recruiters.

What Marketing Resumes Often Miss

Many marketing resumes list channels but not results. "Managed social media" is weak without numbers tied to leads, pipeline, or revenue.

ATS systems also need clear tool and channel terms such as lifecycle marketing, paid search, CRM, and attribution.

Summary Example for Marketing Manager

Bad summary

Experienced marketer with strong communication skills and passion for digital campaigns.
Better summary

Marketing Manager with 6 years of B2B growth experience across paid acquisition, lifecycle, and content. Increased MQL volume by 42% and reduced CAC by 19% through channel mix optimization and conversion-focused experimentation.

ATS-Friendly Skills Section

  • Channels: SEO, SEM, paid social, email, affiliate.
  • Measurement: GA4, attribution models, cohort analysis, funnel reporting.
  • Platforms: HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Ads, Meta Ads Manager.

Use wording from the job post when it matches your background, and group skills so they are easy to scan.

High-Impact Bullet Points

Example marketing bullets

• Rebuilt paid search account structure and landing pages, increasing conversion rate from 2.4% to 3.6% while reducing CPL by 23%.
• Launched lifecycle email program for trial users, raising activation by 16% and improving trial-to-paid conversion by 9%.
• Introduced campaign measurement framework and weekly reporting cadence, reducing budget waste by $11,000 per quarter.
