Quick takeaway: combine writing quality with measurable content outcomes.

What hiring teams look for

For content roles, recruiters check subject relevance, quality of output, and impact on traffic, leads, or conversions.

Use clear language and include metrics from published work whenever possible.

Content writer summary example

adaptable summary example

Content Writer with 4+ years of B2B SaaS experience producing SEO articles, landing pages, and email sequences. Grew non-brand organic traffic by 62% in 10 months through keyword research, content briefs, and editorial optimization.
