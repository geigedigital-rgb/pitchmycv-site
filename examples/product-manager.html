Product Manager Resume Example & Guide

A good PM resume should show ownership, clear decisions, and measurable outcomes. This page gives a format that works for ATS and recruiters.

Why Product Manager Resumes Get Ignored

Many PM resumes list responsibilities but not outcomes. Phrases like "worked with engineering" are too broad.

Show what changed because of your work: activation, retention, conversion, revenue, or delivery speed.

Summary Section That Signals Seniority

Bad summary

Product manager seeking a challenging role where I can use my communication skills and passion for technology.
Better summary

Product Manager with 5 years of experience leading B2B SaaS roadmaps across onboarding, billing, and analytics. Shipped features that increased activation by 18% and reduced churn by 11% through data-backed prioritization and cross-functional execution.

Skills and Keywords for PM ATS Matching

  • Core PM terms: roadmap, prioritization, discovery, user research, A/B testing.
  • Execution terms: sprint planning, backlog grooming, stakeholder alignment, GTM launch.
  • Tooling terms: Jira, Amplitude, Mixpanel, SQL, Figma, Confluence.

Use key terms from the target job description, but only where they reflect real experience.

Achievement Bullet Point Framework

Use this format: action + context + metric + business result.

Example PM bullets

• Led onboarding redesign for self-serve customers, improving trial-to-paid conversion from 7.8% to 11.2% in 2 quarters.
• Introduced product analytics dashboards for activation and retention, cutting decision latency for roadmap planning by 35%.
• Coordinated engineering, design, and sales enablement for a pricing experiment that increased ARPU by 9%.
