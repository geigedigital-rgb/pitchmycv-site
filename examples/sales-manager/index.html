What hiring teams check

Sales hiring teams prioritize measurable performance: quota attainment, deal velocity, average contract value, retention, and team coaching outcomes.

Sales manager summary example

adaptable summary example

Sales Manager with 7 years of B2B SaaS experience leading account executives and SDR teams. Drove 123% average quota attainment over 4 quarters and improved pipeline coverage from 2.1x to 3.4x through territory planning and coaching.

Quota and pipeline bullet samples

Example bullet

“Introduced weekly pipeline hygiene process and stage-exit criteria, reducing sales cycle length by 18% and increasing close rate from 21% to 27%.”

Skills section

Sales leadership: forecasting, coaching, territory design
Execution: outbound strategy, deal review, MEDDICC
Tools: Salesforce, HubSpot, Gong, Outreach

Final checks before applying

Run your resume through the ATS checker and make sure every recent role includes at least one measurable sales result.
