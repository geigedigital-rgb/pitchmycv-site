Quick takeaway: clear metrics and plain language improve both ATS score and recruiter response.

What digital marketing hiring teams expect

Hiring teams look for outcomes, not just channel names. Show growth with metrics such as conversion rate, MQL volume, CAC, pipeline contribution, and revenue impact.

Clear wording and clean structure improve ATS readability and reduce recruiter confusion during first-pass screening.

Digital marketing CV summary example

adaptable summary example

Digital Marketing Manager with 6+ years of B2B and SaaS growth experience across paid search, lifecycle email, and content. Increased qualified lead volume by 42% while reducing CAC by 19% through channel optimization and landing-page testing.
