Top ATS Resume Mistakes

Small mistakes in format and wording can lower your ATS score and reduce recruiter response.

Quick answer: Most ATS problems come from complex design, missing keywords, and weak achievement bullets.

Mistake 1: Design over readability

Complex layouts can break parsing. Use a simple layout so systems can read each section correctly.

Mistake 2: Generic bullets without outcomes

Generic bullets are easy to skip. Replace "responsible for" with action + number + result.

Mistake 3: Missing job-specific keywords

If key skills from the job post are missing, your resume may rank lower even when you are qualified.

Mistake 4: Keyword stuffing

Repeating keywords without context hurts readability. Use keywords only where your real experience supports them.

Mistake 5: No final validation

Always run one final check. Use the ATS checker and review the resume checklist before applying.
