Top ATS Resume Mistakes

Small structural and wording mistakes can drop your resume out of the shortlist before a recruiter sees it.

Mistake 1: Design over readability

Complex layouts with columns and decorative elements often break ATS parsing. Prioritize clarity over visual complexity.

Mistake 2: Generic bullets without outcomes

Recruiters and ranking models prioritize impact. Replace "responsible for" with action + metric + business result.

Mistake 3: Missing job-specific keywords

If required skills are absent or mismatched, your resume can score low despite relevant experience.

Mistake 4: Keyword stuffing

Overloading terms without context hurts readability and trust. Use precise terms only where supported by real experience.

Mistake 5: No final validation

Always run a final scan before applying. Use the ATS checker and review our resume checklist.
